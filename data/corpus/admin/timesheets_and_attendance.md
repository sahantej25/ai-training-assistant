# Timesheets & Attendance (Test-only)

## Who Must Fill Timesheets
- Client-billable roles and select project roles.
- If unsure, confirm with your manager in the first week.

## Frequency
- Weekly submission by Monday 12:00 for previous week.

## What to Record
- Project/task code
- Hours worked
- Brief description of work performed

## Corrections
If you submitted incorrectly, open a ticket under “Timesheet Correction” with the week and details.
