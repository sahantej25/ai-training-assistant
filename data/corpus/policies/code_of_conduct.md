# Code of Conduct (Test-only)

## Expected Behavior
- Treat everyone with respect and professionalism.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, and retaliation.
- Maintain confidentiality of internal information.

## Conflicts of Interest
- Disclose potential conflicts to your manager and People Ops.
- External consulting work requires written approval.

## Use of Company Resources
- Use devices and accounts primarily for work.
- Do not install unauthorized software on company devices.

## Reporting Concerns
- Report concerns via People Ops or the anonymous reporting form.
- Urgent issues should be escalated immediately to your manager.
