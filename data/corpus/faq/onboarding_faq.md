# Onboarding FAQ (Test-only)

## Q: Where do I find internal policies?
All policies are stored in the project wiki under “Policies” and are also indexed by the onboarding assistant.

## Q: What if I can’t access a tool I need?
Create an IT ticket under “Access Request”. If it blocks Day 1 setup, use “Day1 Access Blocker”.

## Q: How do I request leave?
Use the HR portal. For planned leave, request at least 7 days in advance.

## Q: How do I submit expenses?
Use the expense portal and submit within 10 days, with itemized receipts.
