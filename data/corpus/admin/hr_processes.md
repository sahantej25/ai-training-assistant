# HR Processes (Test-only)

## Updating Personal Details
Use the HR portal to update address, phone, emergency contact.

## Requesting Documents
- Employment verification letter: open HR ticket category “Letters”.
- Payslip queries: open HR ticket category “Payroll”.

## Performance Check-ins
- 30/60/90-day check-ins for new employees.
- Quarterly growth conversations.

## Escalation
If your request is urgent and not acknowledged within 1 business day, escalate to your manager and add “URGENT” in the ticket subject.
