# Expense Policy (Test-only)

## Purpose
Define what business expenses are reimbursable and how to submit claims.

## Reimbursable Categories
- Business travel (flight/train, taxi, rideshare)
- Meals during approved travel (within limits)
- Client meetings (with manager approval)
- Work-from-home essentials (one-time allowance; see limits)
- Training/certifications (pre-approved)

## Non-Reimbursable
- Personal travel upgrades without approval
- Alcohol (unless explicitly approved for client events)
- Late fees caused by personal delays
- Family member expenses

## Limits
- Meals during travel: up to 800 per day (local currency) with itemized receipts.
- Work-from-home allowance: up to 5,000 one-time in first 90 days with approval.
- Local commute is not reimbursable unless required for offsite client visit.

## Submission Process
1. Submit within 10 days of incurring the expense.
2. Attach itemized receipt; for travel attach itinerary.
3. Choose correct cost center and project tag.
4. Manager approval required for amounts above 2,000.
