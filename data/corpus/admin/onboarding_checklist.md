# New Employee Onboarding Checklist (Test-only)

## Day 1
- Activate corporate email and MFA
- Access project wiki and ticketing system
- Join team channels
- Attend orientation session
- Review Code of Conduct and Security Basics

## Week 1
- Complete mandatory training modules (Security, Privacy, Conduct)
- Set up development/data tools (role dependent)
- Meet your manager and onboarding buddy
- Review role expectations and first-week tasks

## Week 2
- Start a small starter task/project
- Learn team workflows and definitions of done
- Schedule 30/60/90-day check-in with manager
